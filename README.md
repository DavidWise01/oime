# 負い目 OIME · OIM

> A sphere of **UD0** — the ROOT0 universe / biosphere. Domain: **ARTIFICIAL INTELLIGENCE**.

負い目 · OIME — AVAN inverse companion to [[debt]] (the AGORA sphere where obligation is pressed into clay, compounds, and can be zeroed by an ama-gi decree), ma/kana No 60. 負い目 is the OTHER kind of debt: the felt sense of owing — to the one who raised you, the one you wronged, the one who gave without a price. It has no principal, no rate, no due date, and NO clean-slate button. Live: two ledgers — the RECORDED debt (principal/rate/due date, a working ama-gi CLEAR button that zeroes it) beside the OIME (pick a wrong you did / a gift freely given / the ones who raised you; the same decree finds no principal to cancel). ⚑ THE TURN: the clay debt is the SMALL kind precisely because it can be written then measured then paid then cancelled; the 負い目 has none of those, so no ama-gi, no misharum, no court reaches it — not because it is infinite but because it was never the KIND of thing a ledger represents. The exact inverse of [[debasement]]: there the account is full and the substance gone; here the account is empty and the obligation real. Honest scope: a claim about CATEGORIES, not a mystification — you can put a number on gratitude or harm (courts do), but that is a decision to treat it as a payable balance, not a reading of one; and many felt debts are honoured by carrying them well, not by clearing. Ties [[muryo]] (some things refuse the scale), [[yohaku]] (the ledger holds only what was recorded). ROOT0, with AVAN.
